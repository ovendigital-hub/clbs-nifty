CLBS FRAMEWORK

Context → Level → Bias → Strategy · Intraday Nifty Options Decision Engine

All price / VWAP / value-area inputs use Nifty Futures (which has volume) — not spot.

C · Context

Is the environment tradable?

Time of day, India VIX, events, expiry, global cues. A gate — bad context = no trade.

→
L · Level

Where is price vs the map?

VWAP, Initial Balance, prior-day & prior-week Value Area / POC. No level = no edge.

→
B · Bias

Which way is pressure?

Structure, OI/writing, momentum, PCR, max pain + value acceptance → Bullish / Bearish / Neutral.

→
S · Strategy

When & how to enter?

Timeframe + candlestick at the level → system maps crossing triggers, SL & target from your level map.

Educational framework only — not investment advice. This tool structures a discretionary trading checklist using inputs you provide; it does not predict markets, guarantee outcomes, or constitute a recommendation to buy or sell any security. Options trading carries a high risk of loss. Thresholds (VIX bands, time windows, R:R) are configurable rules of thumb, not rules of the market. Trade your own plan and risk responsibly.

The Central Pivot Range, Floor Pivots, CPR-width day-type forecasting and the seven two-day value-area / CPR relationships are based on concepts from Frank Ochoa's Secrets of a Pivot Boss (and the Money Zone / Market Profile lineage). CPR-width thresholds here are heuristics for Nifty and work best at clear extremes.
